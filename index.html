About me

Background

Hello! My name is Ben Billings. I am 20 years old, live in Columbus, Ohio, and am an aspiring web developer. I currently work at a restaurant in town as a food runner and host; I also work for Search2Close, a title company, as a recording specialist. I am studying web development through the Vanderbilt University Coding Bootcamp. You can find my work below as well as my contact information.

My goals

When I graduated high school in 2020, I didn't have much of a plan for my future. I attended the University of Southern California for one year, where I was studying economics. However, I was feeling unfulfilled, and decided to leave school to pursue other opportunities. Coding was of interest to me because I am typically a logical thinker, and I love problem solving. I also love the idea of being able to create something, so web development seems like the perfect fit for me!
